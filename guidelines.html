SEBI Regulatory Guidelines

Your guide to safe and informed investing.

Investor Do's & Don'ts

Always verify the credentials of any financial advisor or firm through official channels. Never invest based solely on unsolicited advice or promises of guaranteed returns. Be wary of schemes that seem too good to be true, and always read the fine print before investing.

How to Identify Common Scams

Ponzi Schemes

Luring investors with high returns, these schemes pay earlier investors with new investor funds, creating a facade of profit until it collapses. Watch for guaranteed high returns and lack of transparency.

Pump & Dump

Fraudsters artificially inflate a stock's price with misleading statements, then sell their shares once the price peaks, causing losses for other investors. Beware of sudden, hyped-up price increases.

Fake IPOs

Scammers pose as legitimate companies offering pre-IPO shares that don't exist. Always verify an IPO's authenticity through official regulatory filings and be wary of unsolicited offers.
